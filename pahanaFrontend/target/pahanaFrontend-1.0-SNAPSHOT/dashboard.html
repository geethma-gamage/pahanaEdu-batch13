About Pahana Edu

Pahana Edu is a modern bookstore management platform designed to handle billing, inventory, and customer records with ease. We help streamline bookstore operations in Colombo City.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a new customer?

Go to the customer section and click "Add New". Fill in the required information and click save.

Can I edit a bill after it's saved?

No, for auditing purposes, bills cannot be edited once saved. If an error occurs, you can generate a corrected bill.

How do I add a new book to the system?

Navigate to the Items section and click "Add New". Enter the book's name, price, and quantity, then save.

What happens if a customer returns a book?

Currently, returns must be handled manually. You may refund the customer and update inventory accordingly.

How can I view billing history?

Go to the Billing section. Each customer has a history list showing the last 5 bills.

How do I reset my admin password?

If password recovery is enabled, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. If not, contact system support.
