📘 Help & Usage Guidelines - Pahana Edu System

Welcome to the Pahana Edu Bookshop Admin System. This guide will help you understand how to use the system efficiently to manage your bookstore operations.

📄 View & Manage Tenders

Access all current and archived tender documents. Admin users can upload new tenders, update descriptions, and mark tenders as closed after awarding.

👤 Customer Registration & Management

Add new customers with account number, name, address, and contact details. Update or delete records as needed. Duplicate account numbers will be rejected for integrity.

📦 Item & Inventory Management

Add and manage items such as books, stationery, and educational materials. Track stock levels, prices, and item descriptions. Low-stock alerts notify staff to restock in time.

🛒 Billing & Sales

Select a customer and add items to generate a detailed bill. The system automatically calculates totals. Bills can be saved and downloaded as PDFs for printing or record-keeping.

📋 View Past Bills

Easily retrieve past bills by customer name, bill number, or date range. Useful for returns, reporting, and auditing purposes.

📊 Reporting & Statistics

View monthly and yearly sales summaries. Export reports for financial review or inventory planning. Reports include top-selling items and customer purchase trends.

🔐 User Login & Security

Only authorized users can log into the system. Ensure secure credentials are used. Admins can manage user roles and permissions for added security.

⚙️ System Settings

Customize your system settings including tax rates, bill headers, and business information. Changes will reflect on printed documents automatically.
