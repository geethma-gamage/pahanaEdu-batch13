Help & Usage Guidelines

Welcome to the Pahana Edu Admin System. Below are the basic steps to help you get started:

📄 View Tenders

Use this section to view all current and past tender documents related to bookstore operations.

👤 Customer Management

Add, update, or delete customer records. Ensure account numbers are unique and valid.

📦 Item Management

Manage your bookstore inventory including item names, stock levels, and prices.

🖨️ Print Bill

Select customers and items to generate bills. You can also download bills as PDF documents.

📋 View Bills

Review previously generated bills for any customer, sorted by date or bill number.
